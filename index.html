Introduction:

This project visualizes the annual military expenditures, education expenditures, and health expenditures of eleven nations from 2012 to 2017 to compare the trends of their expenditures by time and relationships among countries. To further compare the position of them in the development of a country, GDP will also be taken in comparison.

In the homepage, the general dataset and relationship between spendings are displayed. Each spending has a corresponding section, which has more detailed analysis.
